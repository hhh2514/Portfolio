    BTS

    BTS SIO Option SISR

    Here are the different Skills

    The BTS SIO, abbreviation of Information Systems to Organizations, is a two-year training program aimed at training professionals capable of meeting the information technology needs of businesses.

    The SISR option, for Infrastructure Solutions, Systems and Networks, specifically focuses on the design, implementation, and maintenance of information technology infrastructures.


    Training content :
    Network technologies: Learning network protocols, network architectures, and network management techniques.

    System administration: Management of operating systems, servers, databases, and administration tools.
    Computer security: Data protection, risk management, and implementation of security strategies.

    Virtualization and cloud computing: Use of virtualization technologies and cloud computing services to optimize computer resources.
    Project management: Project management methodologies, planning, monitoring, and evaluation of IT projects.

    Career opportunities :

    -System and network administrator

    -IT support technician

    -System and network engineer

    -IT security consultant

    -IT project manager



    The BTS SIO option SISR offers versatile and practical training, preparing students to quickly integrate into the job market or to continue their studies in the field of information technology.

    E4/E5 Test

    E4 concerns the Design and Maintenance of IT Solutions, while E5 concerns the Production and Provision of Services.

    These tests evaluate students' skills in areas such as IT project management, IT solution design, system and network maintenance, and IT service management.
